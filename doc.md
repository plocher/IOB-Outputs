## IOB-Outputs


PNP Ready: Panelized IOB quad "output board", used for driving LEDS and other devices from the [I2C-7311](/pages/I2C-7311) IO expansion board

Features:

* 5v or 9-12v supply,
* Open Drain outputs
* pins are active low

IO4:

* pin 1: 5vDC
* pin 2: !OUT1
* pin 3: !OUT2
* pin 4: !OUT3
* pin 5: !OUT4
* pin 6: GND
